South Common Farmers Market is the market with heart. Located in the Alberta Research Park at 1903 Parsons Road, behind South Common Cineplex Theatres. Open every Saturday from 10 am - 4 pm, and Sunday from 10am-4pm. Farm fresh meat, veggies, fruit, corn and more. There are over 1500 parking stalls around the market, no two mile walks!! Beautifully trees and surrounding green spaces, its worth to come just for the food, friendly atmosphere and free parking!
